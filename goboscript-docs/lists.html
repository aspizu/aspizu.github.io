goboscript Documentation

Lists

To define a list, first assign to it.

list = [];

delete all of [list v]

add to list

list.add "thing";

add [thing] to [list v]

goboscript will transform list assignments with values into add to list blocks.

list = [1, 2, 3];

delete all of [list v]
add [1] to [list v]
add [2] to [list v]
add [3] to [list v]

delete of list

list.delete 1;

delete [1] of [list v]

insert at list

list.insert 1, "thing";

insert [thing] at [1] of [list v]

replace item of with

list[1] = "thing";

replace item [1] of [list v] with [thing]

goboscript provides compound-assignment operators.

list[1] += 2;
list[1] -= 2;
list[1] *= 2;
list[1] /= 2;
list[1] %= 2;
list[1] &= ".";

replace item [1] of [list v] with ((item [1] of [list v]) + [2])
replace item [1] of [list v] with ((item [1] of [list v]) - [2])
replace item [1] of [list v] with ((item [1] of [list v]) * [2])
replace item [1] of [list v] with ((item [1] of [list v]) / [2])
replace item [1] of [list v] with ((item [1] of [list v]) mod [2])
replace item [1] of [list v] with (join (item [1] of [list v]) [.])

item of list

list[1]

(item [1] of [list v])

item # of list

list.index("thing")

(item # of [thing] in [list v])

length of list

list.length

(length of [list v])

list contains

"thing" in list

<[list v] contains [thing] ?>

show and hide

list.show;
list.hide;

show list [list v]
hide list [list v]

Global Lists

Lists by default are "For this sprite only". To make a list "For all sprites", there are two ways.

Either add the variable name in a lists declaration in stage.gobo.

lists global_list;

or assign to the list in stage.gobo.

onflag {
  global_list = [];
}
